Phoenix Walters is a rising artist from Maryland. Raised by Guyanese immigrant parents, Walters had a knack for music and entertaining from a young age. With influences like 50 Cent, J. Cole, Kanye West, Pharrell, Chris Brown, and Eminem; Phoenix began writing poems and raps at the age of 12. Now at 24, Phoenix Walters is pursuing his dreams of becoming a successful entertainer using his various musical talents along with a strong work ethic, passion and entrepreneurial skills.
